People

OUR IN HOUSE BRAIN TRUST.

Loud+Clear was established in 2011 when three partner agencies merged their distinct strengths to create one of Niagara’s largest full-service marketing and advertising firms. The partnership brought together creative advertising agencies Loud Advertising (founded in 1998) and Alderson & Company (founded in 1994), with the strategic marketing and public relations consultancy, Mitchell Westlake (founded in 2001). The firm’s three agency principals, Chris Alderson, Stan DiFruscio and Erin Mitchell, bring more than 45 years of combined marketing and communications experience to the firm.

Stan DiFruscio

Stan studied Graphic Design and Advertising at Niagara College and George Brown College. After freelancing in Toronto and Vancouver, Stan worked in account management, production, and media buying for Williamson and Lalonde in St. Catharines before launching Loud Advertising in 1998. Since then, Stan has developed a diverse client roster that spans many sectors. Stan’s creativity, “call it like it is” style, high energy, lack of volume control and integrated approach to advertising and design grew his agency into a highly respected firm Province-wide.

Chris Robertson

Leading an agency team since 1994, Chris has worked closely with a wide range of not-for-profit clients. As founder and principal of Alderson and Company, Chris has consistently utilized her talent for clearly defining objectives to identify the communications strategies and tactics that best work to meet those objectives. An analytical nature allows Chris to bridge the gap between intellectual and artistic approaches to communication, ensuring creative cohesion with strategic directives.

ErinMitchell

Erin is an established marketing specialist with a senior management background and consulting experience relevant to marketing, communications, and strategic planning in both the public and private sectors. She holds an MBA from the University of Toronto and a BA and MA in English Literature from Cambridge University. Erin’s wine industry experience includes positions as Marketing Director for Vineland Estates Winery and Marketing Manager for Andres Wines’ Estate Wine Group. As principal of Mitchell Westlake, Erin established herself as a respected marketing consultant.
